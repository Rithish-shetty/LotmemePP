PRIVACY POLICY

No accounts. No tracking. No nonsense. Here's exactly what happens with your data.

LAST UPDATED · APRIL 2026

INTRODUCTION

EchoTap is a reflex and memory arcade game for Android. This policy explains what data is involved when you play. We've written it in plain language — no legal tricks.

Developer: Rithish S R · Contact: nexiasdev@gmail.com

WHAT DATA WE COLLECT

SHORT ANSWER: We collect nothing from you personally.
WE DO NOT COLLECT
  • Your name, email, or any account info
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Access to camera, microphone, or contacts
  • Any personally identifiable information
STORED ONLY ON YOUR DEVICE
  • Your best score (saved locally via AsyncStorage)

This data never leaves your phone. Uninstall the app and it's gone.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

EchoTap shows ads via Google AdMob. Here's what that means for you:

GOOGLE ADMOB

EchoTap is free and supported by ads powered by Google AdMob. Google may use your device's advertising ID to show relevant ads. You can opt out anytime in your phone settings under Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID.

Google's Privacy Policy →

AD TYPES USED
  • Interstitial ads — shown between game sessions
  • Rewarded ads — optional, shown only if you choose to watch

We never show ads during active gameplay.

PERMISSIONS

ANDROID PERMISSIONS USED
  • INTERNET — required to load ads
  • VIBRATE — for tap feedback on buttons

No other permissions are requested or required.

COOKIES AND TRACKING

EchoTap itself has zero tracking, zero analytics, and zero cookies. The only data-related activity is Google AdMob's ad personalisation, which you can opt out of as described above.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

EchoTap is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data at all, this policy applies equally to all users regardless of age.

DATA SECURITY

Since we don't collect your personal data on any server, there is nothing for us to leak or lose. Your best score lives only on your own device. All network requests (for ads) use HTTPS encryption.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If we update this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated through an app update notice on the Google Play Store.
