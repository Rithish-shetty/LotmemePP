Privacy Policy

We keep it simple and honest. Here's exactly what we do with your data — spoiler: almost nothing.

Last updated: March 2026

👋 Hey there!

LotMeme is a meme reels app. We built it to be fun, fast, and respectful of your privacy. This page explains what data is involved when you use the app.

We've written this in plain English — no legal jargon, no tricks.

📦 What data do we collect?

Short answer: basically nothing from you personally.

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name or email address
  • Your location
  • Your contacts, camera, or microphone
  • Any account or login information

Stored only on your own device:

  • Memes you bookmark/save
  • Your in-app preferences (e.g. show/hide captions)

This data never leaves your phone. Uninstall the app and it's gone.

🌐 Third-party services

LotMeme pulls meme content from Reddit and shows ads via Google. Here's what that means for you:

Reddit API

Memes come from Reddit's public API — no Reddit login needed, no private data accessed. Think of it like browsing Reddit without an account. Reddit's privacy policy →

Google AdMob (free version only)

The free version shows ads powered by Google AdMob. Google may use your device's advertising ID to show relevant ads. You can turn this off anytime in your phone settings under Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID. Google's privacy policy →

⭐ Upgrade to Pro to remove all ads completely.

RevenueCat (Pro subscribers only)

If you subscribe to Pro, your purchase goes through Google Play and is managed by RevenueCat. We never see your payment details. RevenueCat's privacy policy →

🍪 Cookies & tracking

LotMeme itself has zero tracking, zero cookies, zero analytics. The only data-related activity is Google AdMob's ad personalisation in the free version, which you can opt out of as mentioned above.

🔒 Is my data safe?

Since we don't collect your personal data on our servers, there's nothing for us to leak. All network requests use HTTPS. Your saved memes live only on your device.

✏️ Changes to this policy

If we ever update this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Major changes will be communicated through an app update notice.
