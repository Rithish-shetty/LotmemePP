01 — Overview

Who We Are

WheelIt – Spin & Decide ("WheelIt", "we", "our", or "us") is a free Android application designed to help users make decisions by spinning a customizable wheel. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

By downloading or using WheelIt, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the application.

Plain English: WheelIt is a simple utility app. We don't want your data — we just want you to make great decisions. 🎡

02 — Data Collection

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the app. Here is a full breakdown:

03 — Usage

How We Use Information

The limited information involved in running WheelIt is used solely for:

  • Saving your custom wheels so they persist between app sessions
  • Displaying your spin history for your own reference
  • Maintaining your daily spin streak and milestone notifications
  • Remembering your app preferences (theme, sound settings)
  • Delivering advertisements through Google AdMob to keep the app free
  • Sending optional daily reminder notifications (only if you grant permission)

We do not analyze, profile, sell, rent, or share your wheel content or decision history with anyone. Your decisions are yours alone.

04 — Advertising

Advertising (Google AdMob)

WheelIt is free to use and is supported by advertising. We use Google AdMob to display ads within the app. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

The types of ads shown in WheelIt include:

  • Interstitial Ads — full-screen ads shown between spins (approximately every 3 spins)
  • Rewarded Ads — optional ads users can watch to unlock premium themes

Google AdMob may collect the following through its own SDK:

  • Google Advertising ID (GAID) — used to serve relevant ads
  • IP address — used for general location (country/region level only)
  • Device model and OS version — for ad compatibility
  • App interaction data — to measure ad performance

⚠️ AdMob data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can review it at policies.google.com/privacy. You can opt out of personalized ads in your Android device settings under Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID.

We never share your wheel content or spin decisions with AdMob or any advertiser. Ad targeting is based solely on data Google collects through its own systems.

05 — Storage

Data Storage & Security

All user-generated content in WheelIt — including saved wheels, spin history, streak data, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using React Native AsyncStorage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to our servers (we have none)
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party

We do not operate any servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure. WheelIt is a fully offline-first application.

Deleting your data: Simply uninstall WheelIt from your device. All locally stored data will be permanently removed.

06 — Children

Children's Privacy

WheelIt is rated Everyone on the Google Play Store and is suitable for all ages. However, we want to be transparent about advertising:

  • WheelIt does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • We do not target advertising specifically at children
  • Google AdMob is configured to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us immediately

If you are using WheelIt in a classroom or educational setting, we recommend enabling your device's "Opt out of Ads Personalization" setting for additional privacy.

07 — Your Rights

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access — Know what data is held about you (in our case, essentially none beyond what's on your device)
  • Right to Delete — Uninstalling the app deletes all local data immediately
  • Right to Opt Out of Personalized Ads — Via Android Settings → Google → Ads
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — Opting out of personalized ads does not reduce app functionality

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or California (CCPA), we comply with applicable data protection regulations. Since we collect no personal data ourselves, most requests are handled simply by uninstalling the app.

GDPR Note: WheelIt does not have a lawful basis to process personal data because we do not process personal data. The only entity processing data for advertising is Google, subject to their own GDPR compliance measures.

Continued use of WheelIt after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
